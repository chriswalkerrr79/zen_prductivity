Privacy Policy for ZenProductivity (PinLab Bot)
Last updated: April 14, 2026

ZenProductivity ("we," "our," or "us") operates the PinLab Bot automation tool. This policy explains how we handle data.

1. Data Collection
The PinLab Bot is a personal automation tool. It does not collect, store, or share any personal data from other users.

2. API Usage
The app uses the Pinterest API to schedule and post content to the user's authorized Pinterest boards. All authentication tokens (Access Tokens) are stored locally on the user's machine and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services
We use Pinterest's official API. Your use of Pinterest through our tool is subject to the Pinterest Privacy Policy.

4. Contact
If you have questions about this personal project, you can contact the developer through this GitHub repository.
